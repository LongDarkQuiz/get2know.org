Aim of the game

Play our customisable trivia game with someone that you would like to get to know better.     You build bespoke replies to our questions and then compete against each other to guess the REAL answers.

Building the perfect answers

No one knows you better than yourself!   Which is why our game gives you full autonomy to customising your answers. You can perfectly reflect your views and personality.

Play for free

Have the first few rounds on us!   You can try ‘ Get 2 Know ’ completely free. Have a go building your profile and enjoy learning more about who your opponent really is!
